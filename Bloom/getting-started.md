# Getting Started

[Player index](README.md) · [Commands](commands.md) · [Actionbar and History](actionbar-history.md)

Bloom rewards everyday gameplay in the background.

## What Bloom can reward

Bloom may pay you for:

- breaking blocks
- placing blocks
- killing mobs
- crafting items
- smelting items
- staying active online over time

Your server can change the exact payout values, categories, and limits.

## What you do not need to do

You do not need to:

- join a job
- choose a class
- open a setup menu
- accept a quest
- keep a command running

Bloom works automatically while you play.

## What can stop a payout

Your server may block payouts in some situations, such as:

- specific worlds
- creative or spectator mode
- breaking player-placed blocks for farming
- extracting from a furnace you do not own
- being AFK too long if movement is required for playtime rewards
- hitting per-minute payout caps

If you feel like something did not pay, staff can usually verify it with Bloom's stats and ledger commands.
